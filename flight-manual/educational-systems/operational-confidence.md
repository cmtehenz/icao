# Operational Confidence

**Confidence is readiness to communicate — not an ICAO exam score.**

This document disambiguates **operational confidence** from **ICAO proficiency level** and from **Mission Readiness** / **Conversation Confidence** learning tools.

Related: [ASSISTANCE-TAXONOMY.md](../ASSISTANCE-TAXONOMY.md) · [learning-tools/conversation-confidence.md](../learning-tools/conversation-confidence.md) · [learning-tools/mission-readiness.md](../learning-tools/mission-readiness.md)

---

## Three different "levels"

| Concept | Meaning | Example label |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| **ICAO proficiency** | Exam outcome | ICAO Level 4, Level 5 |
| **Operational confidence** | Student's readiness to speak in context | Recall confidence ★★★★☆ |
| **Assistance level** | How much support is shown | PA-3, P1-2 |

Never interchange these in student-facing copy.

---

## Operational confidence (CDLI)

Signals Captain uses to **calibrate encouragement and assistance** — not to award licenses.

| Signal | Source |
|--------|--------|
| Recall speed and completeness | Mission Recall |
| Hesitation / filler patterns | Evaluations, instructor memory |
| Recovery after mistake | Session history |
| Consistency across days | Study streak, mission log |
| Self-reported nerves (future) | Optional check-in |

**CDLI capabilities:** Assistance Calibration + Memory Synthesis.

---

## Learning tools (orthogonal)

| Tool | Role |
|------|------|
| **Conversation Confidence** | In-conversation support for Part 1 follow-up (target) |
| **Confidence Gates** | Block PA level-up until demonstrated |
| **Mission Readiness** | Pre-Mock holistic check (target) |
| **Mission Recall confidence** | End of recall leg — readiness for Mock |

CDLI **reads** these tools. It does not replace their specs.

---

## Captain language rules

| Say | Do not say |
|-----|------------|
| "You sound ready for today's mock." | "You are ICAO Level 5." |
| "Strong recall — proceed with confidence." | "Your official score is 92%." |
| "One more pass on readback clarity." | "You failed Part 2." |

Flight Debrief **readiness percent** is a **training estimate** — never an official ICAO score (Section 10).

---

## Relationship to assistance fade

High operational confidence → CDLI may fade assistance faster (IP-07).  
Low confidence after honest effort → temporary support increase, not punishment.

Mission Engine **does not** skip legs because confidence is high.
