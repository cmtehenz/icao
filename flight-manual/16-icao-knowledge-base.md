# Philosophy

The ICAO Knowledge Base is the operational brain of ICAO Delta.

It contains everything the platform knows about ICAO English.

It is not product documentation.

It is aviation knowledge.

Every AI agent consults this knowledge.

Captain Delta teaches from it.

---

# Objective

Centralize all ICAO knowledge in one structured repository.

The Knowledge Base should evolve independently from the application.

Knowledge belongs in the Knowledge Base.

Behavior belongs in the Flight Manual.

Execution belongs in the Prompts.

Intelligence belongs to the AI Brain.

Never mix responsibilities.
