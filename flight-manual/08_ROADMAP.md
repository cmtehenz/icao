# 08 — Roadmap

## Horizon

Long-term product direction for ICAO Delta as a **Digital Flight Academy platform**. This is vision — not sprint plans, status trackers, or implementation schedules.

---

## Platform evolution

### Era 1 — ICAO English (current program)

Complete daily mission flight for SDEA/ICAO exam preparation:

- Guided pronunciation → vocabulary → Part 1 → Part 2 → recall → mock → debrief
- Captain Delta as persistent instructor
- Progressive assistance from full model to solo speech
- Learning memory that informs tomorrow's mission

### Era 2 — Full flight experience

- Unified Begin Flight briefing
- Mission Recall as standard pre-mock leg
- End-of-flight debrief timeline
- Situation board and operational picture synthesis
- Single recording architecture across all legs

### Era 3 — Knowledge Engine

- Curated Knowledge Base replaces AI invention
- Chief Instructor admin panel for phrases, audio, airport notes
- Captain RAG from verified content only
- Real-world asset library (ATC, cockpit video, licensed manuals)

### Era 4 — Platform programs

- Additional training programs on fixed platform (CRM, type ratings, operator SOP)
- Program manifests plug into Mission Engine and Captain Delta
- Shared Learning Engine, separate content

### Era 5 — Instructor intelligence

- Adaptive briefing from memory patterns
- Mission readiness radar before mock
- Flight logbook and milestone tracking
- Passive flight training (Captain Radio, shadow modes) as first-class path

---

## UX north star

ForeFlight-grade mission-focus polish:

- 30 seconds to first speak on every leg
- One coaching surface per event
- Glass-cockpit clarity on recording state
- Mobile-first FAB ergonomics
- Professional aviation aesthetic throughout

---

## Explicitly out of scope

| Item | Reason |
|------|--------|
| Generic ESL grammar course | Violates mission |
| Social leaderboards | Not exam-focused |
| Open ChatGPT chat on home | Violates Captain identity |
| Student chooses daily mission | Violates Mission Engine |
| Auto-play external video | Student controls Real World links |
| Accent elimination training | Clarity over native mimicry |

---

## Success at 12 months

| Measure | Target |
|---------|--------|
| Architecture | Single recording path; Captain as view not controller |
| UX | 30s to first speak on all legs |
| Trust | KDB covers top operational vocabulary with verified context |
| Stability | No idle sync storms; compare-before-write everywhere |
| Outcome | Measurable exam pass-rate correlation with partner schools |
