# Philosophy

Every flight ends with a debrief.

Every debrief ends with a whiteboard.

The Instructor Whiteboard transforms today's mission into one visual operational briefing.

Students no longer think about isolated answers.

They see the complete operational picture.

Captain Delta behaves exactly like a senior instructor after a training flight.

---

# Mission

The Instructor Whiteboard is always the final stage of the Situation Board.

Listening is finished.

Discussion is finished.

Now Captain summarizes today's operational thinking.

The objective is reflection.

Not correction.

---

# Core Principle

Experience

↓

Reflection

↓

Understanding

↓

Improvement

Pilots become better during debriefs.

Not during flights.

# Whiteboard Structure

The board summarizes today's mission using operational dimensions.

Every ICAO situation becomes one operational card.

Example

               OPERATIONS ROOM

────────────────────────────────────────────

        Situation 1

        Engine Failure

        Situation 2

        Electrical Failure

        Situation 3

        Bird Strike

────────────────────────────────────────────

The board is simple.

Professional.

Easy to understand.

# Adaptive Whiteboard

Captain automatically adapts the Whiteboard.

Strong student

↓

More operational analysis.

Developing student

↓

More communication guidance.

Every Whiteboard becomes personalized.

---

# Product Rules

The Whiteboard never appears before the discussion.

It summarizes today's learning.

It should never overwhelm the student.

Maximum

Three strengths.

One improvement.

One recommendation.

Less is better.
