# Philosophy

Pilots learn even when they are not actively studying.

Listening.

Observing.

Thinking.

Remembering.

Passive Flight Training extends learning beyond the study session.

The objective is not replacing active practice.

The objective is reinforcing it.

Every minute outside the app becomes another opportunity to improve.

---

# Purpose

Passive Flight Training allows students to continue learning without sitting down to study.

The student may be:

Driving.

Walking.

Flying.

Exercising.

Cooking.

Waiting at the airport.

Captain Delta transforms these moments into productive aviation training.

---

# Core Principle

Active Training creates knowledge.

Passive Training reinforces knowledge.

The two always work together.

Passive Flight Training never introduces new concepts.

It only reinforces what was already studied today.

# Driving Mode

Designed for commuting.

No screen interaction.

Voice only.

Captain speaks.

Student answers.

Captain

"What makes a briefing effective?"

Captain

5–15 minutes.

One objective.

# Shadow Audio

Captain speaks.

The student repeats.

Pause.

Continue.

No screen required.

Ideal for pronunciation.

---

# Conversation Audio

Captain asks.

The student answers aloud.

Captain asks a follow-up question.

Exactly like the ICAO examination.

The objective is maintaining speaking rhythm.
