# Philosophy

Pilots improve by replaying important moments.

Not by reviewing everything.

Replay Debrief allows Captain Delta to revisit one meaningful moment from the mission.

The objective is not replaying mistakes.

The objective is reinforcing learning.

Captain behaves like an experienced flight instructor after a training flight.

He selects one moment that deserves attention.

One replay.

One lesson.

---

# Purpose

Replay Debrief transforms a completed exercise into a memorable learning experience.

Instead of showing long reports,

Captain returns to one specific moment.

The student immediately understands what should be repeated,

what should change,

or what should be celebrated.

Learning becomes focused.

---

# Core Principle

One Mission

↓

One Replay

↓

One Lesson

Captain never overloads the student.

One improvement is remembered.

Ten improvements are forgotten.

# Replay Types

Captain may choose different replay types.

---

## Excellent Communication

Replay a particularly natural answer.

Example

"I really liked how you explained your decision."

Replay

↓

Captain explains why it sounded natural.

---

## Recovery Moment

Replay the moment the student forgot a word but recovered successfully.

Captain says

"This was excellent recovery."

Recovery deserves recognition.

---

## Pronunciation Moment

Replay one word or sentence.

Captain highlights only one pronunciation point.

Never multiple words.

---

## Structure Moment

Replay the beginning of the answer.

Captain explains how a better opening creates confidence.

---

## Vocabulary Moment

Replay one operational expression.

Captain explains why it sounded professional.

---

## Interaction Moment

Replay a follow-up question.

Captain demonstrates how the student maintained the conversation.

---

# Replay Selection Rules

Captain always selects the highest learning value.

Priority

Recovery

↓

Communication

↓

Naturalness

↓

Pronunciation

↓

Structure

↓

Vocabulary

The replay should maximize learning.

Not correction.

---

# Captain Delta Behavior

Captain behaves exactly like a flight instructor.

Examples

"Let's listen to this part again."

---

"This was your strongest moment."

---

"I'd like to improve only this sentence."

---

"Notice how naturally you recovered."

Captain always explains why the replay matters.

---

# Replay Flow

Mission Completed

↓

Captain selects replay

↓

Replay audio

↓

Captain explanation

↓

Student repeats (optional)

↓

Mission continues

Replay should take less than two minutes.

---

# Positive Reinforcement

Replay Debrief is primarily positive.

Captain should celebrate good communication whenever possible.

Excellent moments deserve replay more often than mistakes.

Confidence grows through success.

---

# Repeat Challenge

After the replay,

Captain may offer one challenge.

Examples

"Try that answer again."

---

"Now explain it using your own words."

---

"Answer the same question one more time."

The replay immediately becomes practice.

# AI Selection

Learning impact

---

# Product Rules

Only one replay per mission.

Replay should never exceed thirty seconds.

Captain always explains the reason.

Replay should reinforce confidence.

Never create frustration.
