# Philosophy

Quick Notes teach students how pilots think.

Pilots do not write sentences.

Pilots capture information.

The objective is not writing.

The objective is remembering.

Quick Notes transform long radio communications into simple operational cues.

Captain Delta teaches students to think like pilots.

Not like language learners.

---

# Purpose

Quick Notes help students capture the essential information during an ICAO communication.

Instead of trying to remember every word,

the student records only the operational information.

The notes become memory triggers.

Not complete transcripts.

Notes are temporary.
