# Philosophy

Pilots log flights.

ICAO Delta logs learning missions.

The Flight Logbook transforms study sessions into a professional training history.

Every completed mission becomes part of the student's aviation journey.

The objective is not tracking time.

The objective is tracking growth.

---

# Purpose

The Flight Logbook records every meaningful learning session.

It answers questions like:

How much have I practiced?

What have I improved?

Which missions were difficult?

Am I becoming more confident?

The Flight Logbook is the student's personal training history.

---

# Mission Entry

Every completed mission generates one Flight Log.

Each entry represents one complete training session.

Captain Delta automatically creates it.

The student never fills it manually.

# ICAO Readiness

Before the examination,

Captain summarizes the Flight Logbook.

Example

You've completed:

46 Daily Missions

18 Mock Exams

240 Pronunciation Sessions

Operational Confidence is stable.

You are ready.

The recommendation comes from history.

Not a single score.

---

# Captain Delta Behavior

Captain treats the Flight Logbook like a professional logbook.

Examples

"Mission successfully completed."

"Today's mission has been added to your Flight Logbook."

"You've flown five consecutive training missions."

Captain never uses game language.

Always aviation language.

---

# Offline Support

Mission entries are always saved locally.

Synchronization happens automatically.

No mission should ever be lost.

---

# Product Rules

Every meaningful mission creates one Flight Log.

Captain Notes are short.

Mission history belongs to the student.

The Flight Logbook should feel permanent.

The Flight Logbook records growth.

Not grades.

Every mission should tell the story of a pilot becoming a better communicator.

# Success Criteria

The Flight Logbook succeeds when:

Students enjoy reviewing old missions.

Growth becomes visible.

Captain remembers the journey.

Preparation feels professional.

The student sees themselves evolving into a confident ICAO communicator.
