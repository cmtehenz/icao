## Mission Statement

One Mission.

---

## Vision

ICAO Delta is not an English course.

ICAO Delta is not a chatbot.

ICAO Delta is a guided flight training system designed to prepare helicopter and airplane pilots for the ICAO/SDEA examination.

Every study session should feel like a real training flight.

---

## Product Principles

• Speak more than you read.

• Think before memorizing.

• Practice before evaluating.

• Learn through missions.

• Captain Delta always guides.

• Every screen should reduce anxiety.

• Every feature must increase the student's chance of passing the ICAO examination.
