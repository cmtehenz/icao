# Vacate Runway

**Catalog ID:** 0028  
**Category:** Airport Operations  
**Priority:** Critical  
**ICAO Parts:** Part 2

## ✈ Mission Brief

Today's lesson covers one of the most time-critical instructions after landing.

Vacate Runway means exit the runway promptly and completely so that other aircraft may land or take off.

Every second you remain on the runway blocks traffic and increases risk.

Professional pilots plan their exit before touchdown and report vacating without being asked.

## 📖 Meaning

Vacate Runway instructs or requires the pilot to leave the runway surface promptly after landing and proceed to the assigned taxiway or exit point.

## 🇧🇷 Portuguese Meaning

Desocupar a pista.

Sair da pista rapidamente após o pouso e seguir para o ponto de saída indicado.

## 🛩 Operational Meaning

When instructed or expected to Vacate Runway, the pilot should:

- plan the exit before landing
- exit the runway at the first safe taxiway unless otherwise instructed
- continue moving until the entire aircraft is clear of the runway
- report vacating with the taxiway designator
- not stop on the runway unless safety requires it

Vacate Runway applies when:

- Tower instructs expedited vacating
- traffic is on short final behind you
- the runway must be cleared for departing traffic
- you are reporting your own runway exit

**Important:** The aircraft is not clear of the runway until all wheels (and in helicopter operations, the landing gear or skids) are past the runway holding markings.

## 🎯 Why ATC Uses It

Tower uses Vacate Runway to:

- clear the runway for landing traffic behind you
- protect departing aircraft waiting for runway access
- maintain airport capacity and traffic flow
- prevent go-arounds caused by runway occupancy
- reduce runway incursion risk

## 🌍 Real Operational Context

You have just landed your H130 at Navegantes on runway zero seven.

Tower has another aircraft on a two-mile final.

Tower: ANAC123, vacate runway zero seven at taxiway Bravo, expedite.

You roll to Bravo without delay, turn off the runway, and report:

Tower, ANAC123 vacating runway zero seven at taxiway Bravo.

Once clear, Ground takes over your taxi instructions.

## 📡 Real ATC Phraseology

1. Tower: ANAC123, vacate runway zero seven at taxiway Bravo.
2. Tower: ANAC123, expedite vacating runway one eight, traffic on short final.
3. Tower: ANAC123, vacate runway two seven left at the next exit.
4. Tower: ANAC123, turn left at Charlie and vacate runway three five.
5. Tower: ANAC123, unable to vacate at Bravo, vacating at Delta.

## 🎙 Professional Pilot Readbacks

1. Vacating runway zero seven at taxiway Bravo, ANAC123.
2. Expediting vacating runway one eight, ANAC123.
3. Vacating runway two seven left at the next exit, ANAC123.
4. Turn left at Charlie, vacating runway three five, ANAC123.
5. Unable Bravo, vacating at Delta, ANAC123.

## 👨‍✈️ Captain Delta Teaching

Picture a busy highway off-ramp.

If you stop in the middle of the exit lane, everyone behind you is blocked.

The runway works the same way.

Plan your exit during the approach briefing.

Know which taxiway you will use before you touch down.

In helicopter operations at airports like Navegantes or Florianópolis, pilots sometimes hover-taxi after landing.

You are still on the runway until you are completely past the holding markings.

Report vacating clearly — Tower may be sequencing an aircraft on short final.

## ❓ Common ICAO Speaking Question

How would you report vacating the runway after landing?

**Good ICAO Level 4 Answer**

I would report to Tower: ANAC123 vacating runway zero nine at taxiway Charlie. I include my callsign, the word vacating, the runway designator, and the taxiway where I am exiting.

## 🇧🇷 Common Brazilian Mistakes

- ❌ Stopping on the runway to complete after-landing checks.  
  ✔ Clear the runway first, then complete checks on the taxiway.

- ❌ Saying "leaving the runway" instead of standard phraseology.  
  ✔ Use vacating runway XX at taxiway YY.

- ❌ Reporting vacating before the aircraft is fully clear.  
  ✔ Report only when completely past the runway holding markings.

- ❌ Ignoring expedite when traffic is on final.  
  ✔ Exit promptly when instructed to expedite vacating.

## 🎯 Captain Challenge

ATC says:

Tower: ANAC123, expedite vacating runway one eight, traffic on a one-mile final.

**Your first task**

Acknowledge and report vacating as you exit.

**Your second task**

Answer this question:

You have turned off the runway but your tail is still over the holding line. Have you vacated?

**Expected ICAO Level 4 Answer**

No. The aircraft is not clear of the runway until the entire aircraft is past the runway holding markings. I must continue until fully clear before reporting vacating.

## 👨‍✈️ Captain Delta Final Debrief

Vacate Runway is about respect for the pilots behind you.

Plan your exit before landing.

Move promptly when cleared.

Report with the taxiway designator.

Never treat the runway as a parking area.

A professional landing ends when you are clear of the runway — not when the wheels touch down.
