# Aft Cargo Door Open

**Catalog ID:** 0283  
**Category:** Emergency  
**Priority:** High  
**ICAO Parts:** Part 2 · Part 3

## ✈ Mission Brief

Today's lesson covers Aft Cargo Door Open — an unsafe or open indication for the aft cargo door, requiring checklist action, possible rejected takeoff or airborne return, and precise ATC communication. ICAO exams often contrast aft with forward cargo door open; using the wrong location is a common speaking error.

For H130 helicopter ICAO Delta academy operations in Brazil, teach aft cargo door open as exam English, then relate it to aft baggage or cabin door unsafe indications on the helicopter. Same radio structure as forward: location, controllability, intentions, return.

Master the distinction: aft versus forward — both are cargo door open, but ATC and maintenance need the correct end of the aircraft.

## 📖 Meaning

Aft Cargo Door Open is an indication or confirmation that the aft cargo door is not closed and locked as required, occurring on the ground, during takeoff, or in flight, and requiring abnormal or emergency procedures, ATC notification, and often rejected takeoff or immediate return / diversion.

## 🇧🇷 Portuguese Meaning

Porta de carga traseira aberta / Aft Cargo Door Open.

Indicação ou confirmação de que a porta de carga traseira não está fechada e travada como necessário, ocorrendo no solo, durante a decolagem ou em voo, e exigindo procedimentos anormais ou de emergência, notificação ao ATC e frequentemente rejeição de decolagem ou retorno / desvio imediato.

## 🛩 Operational Meaning

When aft cargo door open is indicated, the crew should:

- apply the AFM door open / unsafe checklist immediately
- reject takeoff if still on the runway and procedure requires it
- if airborne: avoid cycling the door; return or divert as required
- declare Pan Pan for door open with aircraft controllable; Mayday if separation or severe handling issues develop
- report nature (aft cargo door open), controllability, intentions
- for H130: use aft door / baggage door open for the actual type; keep aft cargo door open for exam English

Aft cargo door open reporting commonly includes:

- aft cargo door open / aft cargo door unsafe
- request immediate return
- aircraft controllable
- possible debris risk if the door fails
- souls and fuel when asked

**Important:** Pronounce aft clearly. Do not say "after cargo door." Aft means rear. Correct ATC if they read back forward when you said aft.

## 🎯 Why ATC Uses This Concept

Controllers respond to aft cargo door open reports to:

- clear priority return or diversion
- alert emergency services to the correct aircraft section
- protect other traffic from possible debris
- request controllability and door status
- coordinate post-landing inspection on the runway if needed

## 🌍 Real Operational Context

Training scenario: climb-out from Confins, aft cargo door open warning. Aircraft controllable.

You transmit:

Pan Pan Pan Pan Pan Pan, Departure, ANAC123, aft cargo door open, aircraft controllable, request immediate return.

Departure: ANAC123, roger Pan Pan, turn right heading one eight zero, descend, expect runway one six, say souls and fuel when able.

You continue:

ANAC123, turning right one eight zero, aft cargo door open, souls ten, fuel two hours thirty, request shortest approach.

If ATC reads back "forward," correct immediately:

ANAC123, negative, aft cargo door open — aft.

## 📡 Real ATC Phraseology

1. Departure: ANAC123, roger Pan Pan aft cargo door open, turn right heading one eight zero, cleared to return.
2. Approach: ANAC123, confirm aft cargo door, say controllability.
3. Tower: ANAC123, runway cleared, services standing by for aft fuselage.
4. Approach: ANAC123, say souls on board and fuel remaining.
5. Tower: ANAC123, after landing hold position for inspection.

## 🎙 Professional Pilot Readbacks

1. Pan Pan Pan Pan Pan Pan, ANAC123, aft cargo door open, request immediate return.
2. ANAC123, turning right one eight zero, descending, returning.
3. ANAC123, negative, aft cargo door open — aft, aircraft controllable.
4. ANAC123, souls ten, fuel two hours thirty.
5. ANAC123, will hold on the runway for inspection, ANAC123.

## 👨‍✈️ Captain Delta Teaching

Aft means rear. Forward means front. On the exam, that single word separates a good answer from a confused one.

Checklist. Pan Pan. Return. Say aft cargo door open. Correct wrong readbacks.

Brazilian pilots may say "porta traseira aberta." Prefer aft cargo door open in ICAO English. Avoid "after door" or "back door" on the exam.

In the H130, map aft to your real aft baggage or cabin door. Keep systems honest. Keep phraseology sharp.

If the door leaves the aircraft, it is no longer "open" — it is separated. Change the words. Upgrade the urgency.

## ❓ Common ICAO Speaking Question

You receive an aft cargo door open warning after takeoff. What do you do and what do you say?

**Good ICAO Level 4 Answer**

I would apply the checklist, declare Pan Pan stating aft cargo door open and that the aircraft is controllable, and request an immediate return. I would correct ATC if they read back forward instead of aft, report souls and fuel when able, and land with services standing by. If the door separated, I would declare Mayday and report cargo door separated.

## 🇧🇷 Common Brazilian Mistakes

- ❌ Saying "after cargo door" instead of aft.  
  ✔ Aft cargo door open — aft means rear.

- ❌ Omitting aft/forward so ATC assumes the wrong door.  
  ✔ Always specify aft or forward.

- ❌ Not correcting a wrong ATC readback.  
  ✔ Negative, aft cargo door open — aft.

- ❌ Inventing H130 aft jet cargo holds.  
  ✔ Exam English yes; type systems factual.

## 🗣 Pronunciation Coaching

**Target Phrase:** Pan Pan Pan Pan Pan Pan, ANAC123, Aft Cargo Door Open, Request Immediate Return

**Pronunciation:** PAN pan PAN pan PAN pan, AY-nak WUN TOO THREE, AFT KAR-go DOR OH-pen, reh-KWEST ih-MEE-dee-it ree-TURN

**Word Stress**

- Aft → AFT (one syllable, clear final T)
- Cargo → KAR-go
- Door → DOR
- Open → OH-pen
- Negative → NEG-uh-tiv (for corrections)

Practice:

Aft cargo door open... negative, aft... request immediate return.

Together:

Pan Pan Pan Pan Pan Pan, ANAC123, aft cargo door open, aircraft controllable, request immediate return.

## 🧠 Memory Trick

**AFT** — **A**nnounce aft clearly, **F**ly the return, **T**ell ATC if they say forward — correct them.
