# Squawk Ident

**Catalog ID:** 0106  
**Category:** ATC Phraseology  
**Priority:** Critical  
**ICAO Parts:** Part 2

## ✈ Mission Brief

Today's lesson covers Squawk Ident — a secondary surveillance radar instruction to press the SPI button on the transponder for positive identification.

Squawk Ident causes the aircraft's return to briefly highlight on the radar display, allowing ATC to confirm exactly which blip is your aircraft.

In the H130, radar identification is routine at Brazilian airports including Congonhas, Curitiba, and São Paulo area airspace.

You must respond immediately, confirm when done, and understand why the instruction matters for radar separation services.

This lesson prepares you for ICAO Part 2 phraseology and transponder operations.

## 📖 Meaning

Squawk Ident is an ATC instruction directing the pilot to activate the IDENT function on the transponder, producing a special position identification pulse visible to the controller on radar.

## 🇧🇷 Portuguese Meaning

Aperte o botão IDENT do transponder.

Instrução do controle para o piloto acionar a função IDENT do transponder, destacando o símbolo da aeronave no radar do controlador.

## 🛩 Operational Meaning

When Squawk Ident is received, the pilot should:

- press the IDENT button on the transponder immediately
- confirm with "Ident" when the action is complete
- ensure the transponder is in ALT or Mode C before ident
- wait for controller confirmation of radar identification
- not press ident unless instructed by ATC

Squawk Ident operations commonly include:

- positive radar identification at initial contact
- re-identification after frequency change
- identification after transponder code assignment
- confirmation of aircraft position during unusual situations

**Important:** Never press IDENT without being instructed. It exists for ATC use only and unauthorized activation can confuse radar displays.

## 🎯 Why ATC Uses It

ATC issues Squawk Ident to:

- positively identify a specific aircraft among several returns
- confirm correct squawk code was entered
- establish radar identification after initial contact
- re-identify aircraft after a transponder anomaly
- confirm position in complex traffic environments

## 🌍 Real Operational Context

You are climbing out of Congonhas in your H130 after departure.

São Paulo Approach calls:

ANAC123, radar contact, squawk ident.

You press the IDENT button and report:

Ident, ANAC123.

Approach confirms:

ANAC123, identified, five miles south of Congonhas, climb and maintain four thousand.

The exchange takes seconds — but it locks your radar identity for the entire flight.

## 📡 Real ATC Phraseology

1. Approach: ANAC123, squawk ident.
2. Approach: ANAC123, squawk two four zero zero and ident.
3. Radar: ANAC123, confirm squawk, then ident.
4. Approach: ANAC123, re-squawk four five six zero and ident.
5. Approach: ANAC123, ident received, radar identified, five miles north of Congonhas.

## 👨‍✈️ Captain Delta Teaching

Squawk Ident is one instruction with one action.

Press. Report. Done.

The sequence:

Hear ident. Press IDENT. Say "Ident, ANAC123."

Brazilian pilots sometimes hesitate, unsure which button to press or when to confirm.

The IDENT button is on the transponder. Press it once. Confirm immediately.

In the H130, the transponder must be in ALT mode for Mode C altitude data to reach ATC with the ident.

Never press ident on your own initiative — only when instructed.

Train the immediate response until it becomes reflex.

## ❓ Common ICAO Speaking Question

What do you do when ATC instructs squawk ident and how do you confirm?

**Good ICAO Level 4 Answer**

When ATC instructs squawk ident, I immediately press the IDENT button on the transponder and confirm by saying "Ident" followed by my callsign. This allows the controller to positively identify my aircraft on radar. I do not press ident unless instructed, as unauthorized activation can create confusion on the radar display.

## 🇧🇷 Common Brazilian Mistakes

- ❌ Pressing IDENT before receiving the instruction.  
  ✔ Wait for ATC to say squawk ident.

- ❌ Not confirming after pressing the button.  
  ✔ Always report: Ident, ANAC123.

- ❌ Saying "Roger, ident" without pressing the button.  
  ✔ Press first, then confirm.

- ❌ Having the transponder in STANDBY when ident is requested.  
  ✔ Ensure transponder is in ALT mode before departure.
