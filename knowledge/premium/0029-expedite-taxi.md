# Expedite Taxi

**Catalog ID:** 0029  
**Category:** Ground Operations  
**Priority:** High  
**ICAO Parts:** Part 2

## ✈ Mission Brief

Today's lesson covers a ground instruction that signals urgency without panic.

When Ground says Expedite Taxi, they need you to move faster on the movement area — safely, but without unnecessary delay.

This is not permission to rush blindly.

It is a request to increase taxi pace because traffic flow, runway sequencing, or another aircraft's needs require it.

Professional pilots expedite while maintaining situational awareness and surface safety.

## 📖 Meaning

Expedite Taxi instructs the pilot to increase taxi speed and proceed without delay to the assigned destination on the movement area.

## 🇧🇷 Portuguese Meaning

Acelere o táxi.

Desloque-se sem demora até o destino indicado, mantendo segurança na superfície.

## 🛩 Operational Meaning

When ATC issues Expedite Taxi, the pilot should:

- increase taxi speed within safe limits for the surface and aircraft type
- proceed directly on the assigned route without stopping unnecessarily
- remain alert for other traffic, vehicles, and surface hazards
- report reaching the destination or holding point as required
- advise ATC if unable to expedite due to safety concerns

Expedite Taxi is commonly used when:

- a departure slot is time-critical
- landing traffic requires the runway to be cleared
- another aircraft is holding for the same taxiway
- ATC is sequencing multiple departures

**Important:** Expedite means move promptly — not recklessly. You must still comply with hold short instructions and avoid runway incursions.

## 🎯 Why ATC Uses It

Ground Control uses Expedite Taxi to:

- maintain efficient traffic flow on the surface
- coordinate runway sequencing between arrivals and departures
- reduce delays for following traffic
- clear the movement area for priority aircraft
- meet departure time constraints

## 🌍 Real Operational Context

You are taxiing your H130 at Florianópolis for departure on runway three one.

Ground needs to clear the taxiway for an arriving aircraft.

Ground: ANAC123, expedite taxi to runway three one via Alpha.

You increase your taxi pace safely along Alpha and report:

ANAC123 expediting taxi to runway three one.

You reach the holding point without unnecessary stops, and Tower clears you for departure shortly after.

## 📡 Real ATC Phraseology

1. Ground: ANAC123, expedite taxi to runway three one.
2. Ground: ANAC123, expedite taxi to holding point Charlie via Bravo.
3. Ground: ANAC123, expedite taxi to the ramp, traffic waiting.
4. Ground: ANAC123, expedite taxi, contact tower at holding point Delta.
5. Ground: ANAC123, expedite taxi to runway one eight, hold short runway two seven.

## 👨‍✈️ Captain Delta Teaching

Imagine you are in a hospital corridor and a nurse says, "Please hurry — the doctor is waiting."

You walk faster, but you do not run into other people.

Expedite Taxi works the same way.

Move with purpose.

Do not stop for nonessential tasks.

But never sacrifice safety for speed.

In Brazil, pilots sometimes confuse expedite with emergency authority.

Expedite is a normal ATC instruction for traffic flow — not a Mayday situation.

If you cannot expedite safely due to debris, construction, or a tight turn in your H130, tell Ground immediately.

## ❓ Common ICAO Speaking Question

What should you do when ATC instructs you to expedite taxi?

**Good ICAO Level 4 Answer**

I should increase taxi speed within safe limits and proceed to the assigned destination without unnecessary delay. I must still comply with hold short instructions and maintain situational awareness. If I cannot expedite safely, I should advise ATC immediately.

## 🇧🇷 Common Brazilian Mistakes

- ❌ Ignoring expedite and continuing at normal taxi pace.  
  ✔ Increase pace promptly when instructed, within safe limits.

- ❌ Treating expedite as permission to cross a runway without clearance.  
  ✔ Hold short instructions still apply during expedited taxi.

- ❌ Saying "going faster" instead of standard phraseology.  
  ✔ Use expediting taxi to runway XX.

- ❌ Rushing without scanning for traffic and surface hazards.  
  ✔ Expedite safely — awareness does not decrease.

## 🗣 Pronunciation Coaching

**Pronunciation:** EK-speh-dy-ting TAK-see too RUN-way three ONE

**Word Stress**

- Expedite → first syllable (EK-speh-dyt)
- Expediting → second syllable (ek-SPEH-dy-ting)

Practice:

Expedite... taxi...

Together:

ANAC123 expediting taxi to runway three one.

Avoid rushing the transmission — speak clearly even when expediting.

## 🎯 Captain Challenge

ATC says:

Ground: ANAC123, expedite taxi to runway three one via Alpha, hold short of runway two seven.

**Your first task**

Read back the instruction as a professional pilot.

**Your second task**

Answer this question:

You see debris on taxiway Alpha ahead. Should you continue expediting?

**Expected ICAO Level 4 Answer**

No. If I cannot expedite safely due to debris or any hazard, I should advise Ground immediately. Expedite does not override safety. I would report the debris and request instructions.

## 👨‍✈️ Captain Delta Final Debrief

Expedite Taxi is ATC asking you to help the traffic flow.

Respond promptly.

Move with purpose.

Keep scanning the surface.

Hold short when required.

If you cannot comply safely, speak up immediately.

Great pilots are fast on the ground and safe on the ground — never one without the other.
