# Maintain Present Heading

**Catalog ID:** 0017  
**Category:** ATC Phraseology  
**Priority:** Critical  
**ICAO Parts:** Part 2 · Part 3

## ✈ Mission Brief

Today's lesson explains a subtle but important ATC instruction.

Unlike Fly Heading, which tells you to turn to a new heading,

Maintain Present Heading means:

Do not turn. Continue flying exactly the heading you already have.

This instruction is commonly used during departures, arrivals, and radar vectoring.

## 📖 Meaning

Maintain Present Heading instructs the pilot to continue flying the current magnetic heading until receiving another ATC clearance.

## 🇧🇷 Portuguese Meaning

Mantenha o rumo atual.

Continue voando exatamente no rumo em que a aeronave já está.

## 🛩 Operational Meaning

When ATC says Maintain Present Heading, the pilot should:

- make no heading changes
- continue on the current magnetic heading
- maintain assigned altitude and speed
- await further instructions

This differs from Fly Heading, which requires turning to a new heading.

## 🎯 Why ATC Uses It

Controllers issue Maintain Present Heading to:

- preserve radar separation
- stabilize traffic flow
- delay turns until traffic clears
- simplify sequencing
- avoid conflicting aircraft

## 🌍 Real Operational Context

You're climbing after departure.

Departure intends to vector you left.

However, another aircraft is crossing ahead.

Instead of issuing the turn immediately, ATC says:

Departure: ANAC123, maintain present heading.

You continue straight ahead.

After the traffic passes:

Departure: ANAC123, turn left heading zero six zero.

Now you begin the turn.

## 📡 Real ATC Phraseology

1. Departure: ANAC123, maintain present heading.
2. Approach: ANAC123, maintain present heading due traffic.
3. Departure: ANAC123, maintain present heading, expect vectors shortly.
4. Center: ANAC123, maintain present heading and maintain five thousand.
5. Approach: ANAC123, maintain present heading until advised.

## 🎙 Professional Pilot Readbacks

1. Maintaining present heading, ANAC123.
2. Present heading due traffic, ANAC123.
3. Maintaining present heading, expecting vectors, ANAC123.
4. Maintaining present heading and five thousand, ANAC123.
5. Maintaining present heading until advised, ANAC123.

## 👨‍✈️ Captain Delta Teaching

Imagine you're driving through an intersection.

Just before you turn, a police officer raises a hand and says:

"Keep going straight."

He doesn't tell you where you'll turn next.

He simply tells you not to turn yet.

That's exactly what Maintain Present Heading means.

Professional pilots understand that doing nothing can be just as important as making a maneuver.

Sometimes the safest action is simply to continue exactly as you are.

## ❓ Common ICAO Speaking Question

What is the difference between "Maintain Present Heading" and "Fly Heading 090"?

**Good ICAO Level 4 Answer**

Maintain Present Heading means I continue flying my current heading without turning. Fly Heading 090 instructs me to turn, if necessary, and establish heading zero niner zero.

## 🇧🇷 Common Brazilian Mistakes

- ❌ Beginning a turn because you expect one.  
  ✔ Continue straight.

- ❌ Confusing Present Heading with Resume Own Navigation.  
  ✔ Navigation responsibility does not change.

- ❌ Reading back only: Roger.  
  ✔ Correct: Maintaining present heading, ANAC123.
