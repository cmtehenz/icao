# Runway Excursion

**Catalog ID:** 0051  
**Category:** Airport Operations  
**Draft:** Premium batch-03 — working document (not consumed by Captain)

## Meaning

Aircraft departing the side of the runway surface during takeoff or landing.

## Operational Meaning

Post-event report to Tower; may block runway and require emergency services.

## When Used

Wet runway, crosswind, or hydroplaning causes aircraft to leave paved surface.

## Who Uses It

Pilots report after stopping; Tower closes runway and alerts rescue.
