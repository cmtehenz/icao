# Runway Overrun

**Catalog ID:** 0052  
**Category:** Airport Operations  
**Draft:** Premium batch-03 — working document (not consumed by Captain)

## Meaning

Aircraft passing beyond the end of the runway during landing or rejected takeoff.

## Operational Meaning

Serious runway safety event; crew reports position; runway closed for inspection.

## When Used

Late braking, wet runway, or high approach speed causes aircraft to exceed runway end.

## Who Uses It

Pilots report to Tower; ATC alerts emergency services and closes runway.
