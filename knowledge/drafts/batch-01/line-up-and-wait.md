# Line Up and Wait

**Catalog ID:** 0003  
**Category:** ATC Phraseology  
**Source level:** researched  
**Draft:** Premium batch-01 — working document (not consumed by Captain)

## Meaning

Enter the departure runway, align with the runway, and wait for takeoff clearance.

*Portuguese: Alinhar na pista e aguardar.*

## Operational Meaning

ATC uses it when the aircraft may enter the runway, but takeoff clearance cannot be issued yet. You are on the runway but not cleared for takeoff — position for imminent departure while landing traffic clears or wake turbulence dissipates.

## When Used

When departure is imminent but Tower must wait for landing traffic to vacate, wake turbulence separation, crossing traffic, or runway condition advisories before issuing takeoff clearance.

## Who Uses It

Tower controllers issue line up and wait; pilots read back the full instruction including runway designator and wait in position until cleared for takeoff.

## Real ATC Phraseology

- PT-ABC, line up and wait runway one eight.
- PT-ABC, runway two seven, line up and wait.
- PT-ABC, line up and wait behind landing traffic.
- PT-ABC, runway one eight shortened, line up and wait.
- PT-ABC, line up and wait, traffic departing crossing runway.

## Real Pilot Readbacks

- Line up and wait runway one eight, PT-ABC.
- Runway two seven, lining up and waiting, PT-ABC.
- Line up and wait behind landing traffic, PT-ABC.
- Runway one eight shortened, line up and wait, PT-ABC.
- Line up and wait, PT-ABC.

## Common ICAO Speaking Questions

- What is the difference between line up and wait and cleared for takeoff?
- Why is a correct readback critical after line up and wait?
- What must you not do after lining up and waiting?

## Common Brazilian Mistakes

- Starting takeoff after line up and wait.
- Thinking after landing traffic is a takeoff clearance.
- Not reading back the runway number.

## Pronunciation Coaching

- LINE up and WAIT — make both line and wait clear.
- Four distinct words at a steady pace.
- Do not clip and — it matters in the readback.

## Captain Teaching Notes

This is NOT takeoff clearance. The pilot may enter the runway and align, but must wait until hearing cleared for takeoff. ICAO phraseology replaced position and hold with line up and wait.

## Memory Trick

Line up = enter runway. Wait = do not take off.
