# Radar Vectors

**Catalog ID:** 0010  
**Category:** Navigation  
**ICAO parts:** 2, 3  
**Source level:** researched  
**Draft:** Premium batch-01 — working document (not consumed by Captain)

## Meaning

ATC headings issued to guide an aircraft toward a route, approach, or traffic sequence.

*Portuguese: Vetoração radar.*

## Operational Meaning

Instead of navigating independently, the pilot follows ATC headings until instructed otherwise. While receiving radar vectors, ATC is responsible for navigation guidance. The pilot flies assigned headings until instructed to resume own navigation.

## When Used

On departure, en route sequencing, weather deviations, GPS failure, emergency handling, or when Approach vectors aircraft to intercept the final approach course or ILS.

## Who Uses It

Departure, Approach, Center, and Tower assign headings and vectors; pilots read back and fly assigned headings until resume own navigation or a direct clearance.

## Real ATC Phraseology

- PT-ABC, turn left heading 270.
- PT-ABC, fly heading 180.
- PT-ABC, vectors for the ILS.
- PT-ABC, turn right heading 090.
- PT-ABC, radar vectors to final.

## Real Pilot Readbacks

- Left heading 270.
- Heading 180.
- Vectors ILS.
- Right heading zero niner zero.
- Radar vectors to final acknowledged.

## Common ICAO Speaking Questions

- Why does ATC provide radar vectors?
- What would you read back after receiving a heading assignment?
- When does radar vectoring end?

## Common Brazilian Mistakes

- Turning before reading back the heading.
- Mixing heading with course or track.
- Forgetting resume own navigation when vector service terminates.

## Pronunciation Coaching

- RAD-ar VEC-tors — clear stress on both words.
- Say headings digit by digit: zero niner zero, not ninety.
- Vocabulary uses Helicopter PT-ABC, fly heading zero nine zero and Turn left heading two seven zero, vectors to the ILS.

## Captain Teaching Notes

While receiving radar vectors, ATC is responsible for navigation guidance. The pilot flies assigned headings until instructed to resume own navigation. Students material uses N123IJ requesting vectors for the final approach course and On your vectors to final.

## Memory Trick

You fly headings. ATC flies the route.
