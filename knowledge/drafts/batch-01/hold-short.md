# Hold Short

**Catalog ID:** 0002  
**Category:** ATC Phraseology  
**Source level:** researched  
**Draft:** Premium batch-01 — working document (not consumed by Captain)

## Meaning

Stop before a runway, taxiway, or specified point and do not cross it.

*Portuguese: Manter-se antes do ponto indicado / não cruzar.*

## Operational Meaning

Used mainly on the ground to prevent runway incursions and protect active runways or movement areas. Hold short is a safety-critical instruction — the pilot must read it back in full and stop at the holding point. Never assume a taxi clearance allows runway crossing unless the clearance explicitly includes it.

## When Used

During taxi when Ground or Tower needs to keep aircraft clear of an active runway, intersecting taxiway, or movement area until further clearance is issued.

## Who Uses It

Ground and Tower controllers issue hold short instructions; pilots read back the full clearance including the runway or taxiway designator and stop before the hold-short line.

## Real ATC Phraseology

- PT-ABC, hold short of runway one eight.
- PT-ABC, taxi via Alpha, hold short runway two seven.
- PT-ABC, hold short of taxiway Bravo.
- PT-ABC, hold short of the active runway.
- PT-ABC, cross runway zero niner, hold short runway one eight.

## Real Pilot Readbacks

- Holding short runway one eight, PT-ABC.
- Taxi via Alpha, holding short runway two seven, PT-ABC.
- Holding short taxiway Bravo, PT-ABC.
- Holding short active runway, PT-ABC.
- Crossing zero niner, holding short one eight, PT-ABC.

## Common ICAO Speaking Questions

## Common Brazilian Mistakes

- Answering only roger instead of reading back hold short.
- Saying holding position but missing the runway number.
- Crossing a hold-short line without explicit clearance.

## Pronunciation Coaching

- HOLD short — make hold clear and strong.
- Equal stress on both words with a crisp pause between them.
- Read runway numbers digit by digit.

## Captain Teaching Notes

This is a safety-critical instruction. The student must read it back in full. Never assume a taxi clearance allows runway crossing unless the clearance explicitly includes it. Many runway incursions start with an incomplete hold short readback.

## Memory Trick

Hold short = stop before crossing.
