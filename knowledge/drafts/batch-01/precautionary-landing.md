# Precautionary Landing

**Catalog ID:** 0030  
**Category:** Emergency  
**Draft:** Premium batch-01 — working document (not consumed by Captain)

## Meaning

Landing when a potential emergency exists but immediate danger is not yet confirmed.

## Operational Meaning

Crew lands at a suitable airport to inspect a problem — less urgent than emergency landing.

## When Used

Doubtful gear indication, unusual smell, or vibration that requires ground inspection.

## Who Uses It

Pilots inform ATC; ATC may assign holding or vectors without full emergency response.
